Bridge — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-04-25 · Sus.AI LLC · Roswell, GA

Bridge is an SMS-based daily check-in companion service for older adults, operated by Sus.AI LLC ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Plain English, no surprises.

1. What we collect

From the family member who signs up ("Sponsor")

  • Name and email address.
  • Payment method (processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers).
  • Name and mobile phone number of the person to be enrolled ("Recipient").
  • Recipient's preferred check-in time and basic preferences.

From the Recipient via SMS

  • Inbound text messages sent to our 10DLC number.
  • Timestamp, message length, and a record of any safety-flag triggers (e.g., distress, scam-bait phrases).
  • Aggregate response patterns (frequency, time-of-day) used to tune outreach cadence.

Automatically

  • Carrier delivery status (delivered / failed) from Twilio.
  • Service logs (errors, latency) — no message content in operational logs beyond redacted IDs.

2. How we use it

  • To send daily check-in messages to the Recipient.
  • To generate replies via Kimi K2.6 (operated by Moonshot AI / OpenRouter) — see "Third parties" below.
  • To produce a daily digest for the Sponsor summarizing the Recipient's mood, key mentions, and any safety flags.
  • To detect potential safety concerns (medical distress, scam attempts) and notify the Sponsor.
  • To bill the Sponsor and provide customer support.

We do not use Recipient messages for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating Bridge.

3. Third parties

Bridge relies on a small number of vendors:

  • Twilio — message delivery and 10DLC compliance. Twilio Privacy
  • OpenRouter / Moonshot AI (Kimi K2.6) — language model that generates replies. OpenRouter Privacy
  • Stripe — payment processing for Sponsors. Stripe Privacy
  • Anthropic — used for daily digest generation in some configurations. Anthropic Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any party, vendor or otherwise.

4. Storage and retention

  • Conversation logs are stored on infrastructure controlled by Sus.AI LLC, encrypted at rest.
  • Default retention: 90 days rolling for full message content; aggregate counts kept indefinitely for service improvement.
  • Sponsors may request a data export or full deletion at any time by emailing atw.linked+bridge@gmail.com. Deletion is honored within 30 days.
  • Recipients may text STOP at any time to immediately cease all messaging; their conversation history is retained per the 90-day rolling window unless deletion is also requested.

5. SMS terms (10DLC)

  • Bridge sends SMS only to phone numbers explicitly opted in via the consent SMS flow. Recipients must reply YES after Sponsor enrollment to begin receiving messages.
  • Reply STOP, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, or CANCEL to end. Confirmation reply will follow; no further messages will be sent.
  • Reply HELP or INFO for assistance.
  • Message frequency: typically 1–3 per day. Message and data rates may apply per your carrier plan.
  • Carriers supported: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and most US MVNOs.

6. Children's privacy

Bridge is intended for adults aged 55 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor's data has been collected, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (CCPA, GDPR, etc.), you may have rights to access, correct, port, or delete your data. We honor these rights regardless of jurisdiction. To exercise any of them, email atw.linked+bridge@gmail.com.

8. Security

  • Conversation content encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
  • API keys stored in macOS Keychain and rotated per access need.
  • No production data on shared cloud services beyond named third-party vendors above.
  • Incident response: any confirmed breach affecting Sponsor or Recipient data will be reported within 72 hours via the Sponsor's email on file.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle data, we will email all active Sponsors and update the "Effective" date above. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance.
